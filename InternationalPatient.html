Our Services for International Patients

At HealthFirst Medical Center, we understand the unique needs of international patients seeking high-quality, affordable, and compassionate healthcare. Our dedicated International Patient Services team ensures a smooth and stress-free medical journey from your first inquiry to your return home. We provide personalized care, multilingual support, and full assistance with travel, stay, and treatment arrangements to make your experience as comfortable and successful as possible.

  • Visa Invitation Letter Support
  • Airport Pickup & Drop
  • Online Medical Consultation
  • Customized Treatment Packages
  • Language Translation Assistance
  • Help with Local Accommodation
  • Priority Appointments with Specialists
  • Dedicated International Helpdesk
  • Currency Exchange Guidance
  • Daily Progress Updates
  • Post-Treatment Travel Clearance
  • Online Follow-Up Services
  • Assistance with Local SIM Card

Treatment Process

1. Initial Inquiry

Patients contact us via email, phone, or online form to begin their medical journey.

2. Medical Evaluation

Our experts assess medical reports and suggest the best treatment plan.

3. Treatment Plan & Cost

We share estimated cost, procedure details, and expected duration.

4. Travel & Visa Assistance

Support for medical visa letters, accommodation, and flight planning.

5. Arrival & Registration

Warm welcome at the airport and smooth hospital admission process.

6. Diagnosis & Treatment

Patient undergoes required investigations, surgeries, or therapy sessions.

7. Recovery & Discharge

Post-treatment care and discharge with follow-up instructions.

8. Post-Treatment Follow-up

Continued support through online consultation after returning home.
