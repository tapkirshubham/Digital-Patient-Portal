Comprehensive Blood Component Services

We offer a full spectrum of blood components, including packed red blood cells, fresh frozen plasma, platelet concentrates, and cryoprecipitate. Our advanced component separation techniques ensure that patients receive the most appropriate and effective transfusion therapy.


Single Donor Platelet (SDP) Apheresis

Understanding the critical need for platelets in various medical treatments, our facility provides Single Donor Platelet (SDP) apheresis services. This process yields a higher concentration of platelets from a single donor, enhancing transfusion efficacy and reducing patient exposure to multiple donors.


Leucoreduction for Enhanced Safety

To minimize transfusion-related reactions, we employ leucoreduction techniques that effectively remove white blood cells from donated blood. This process enhances the safety and effectiveness of transfusions, aligning with global best practices.


Blood Irradiation Services

For immunocompromised patients, our blood irradiation services play a vital role in preventing Transfusion-Associated Graft versus Host Disease (TA-GvHD). By irradiating blood components, we ensure safer transfusions for vulnerable patient populations.


Nucleic Acid Testing (NAT) for Infection Screening

We prioritize patient safety by implementing Nucleic Acid Testing (NAT) to screen all donated blood for infections such as HIV, Hepatitis B, and Hepatitis C. This advanced testing method allows for earlier detection compared to traditional screening techniques, significantly reducing the risk of transfusion-transmitted infections.


Therapeutic Services

Our Blood Center extends its expertise to therapeutic procedures, including Therapeutic Phlebotomy for conditions like polycythemia and Therapeutic Plasma Exchange (TPE) for patients with disorders such as Guillain-Barré syndrome and myasthenia gravis. These services are performed with precision and care, adhering to the highest medical standards.


Community Engagement and Blood Donation Drives

Recognizing the importance of community involvement, we actively organize blood donation drives and awareness campaigns. Our team engages with local organizations and volunteers to maintain a steady and safe blood supply, fostering a culture of altruism and community support.
